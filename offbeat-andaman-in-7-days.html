    Tour Highlights

    Amazing Andaman Tour
    Andaman island has Amazing unexplored locations, These locations will disconnect you from the hustle and bustle of the city and create memories for lifetime.
    The Amazing Beaches
    Enjoy the journey of 7 days, explore the Amazing white coastline, clear water beaches, spend some time on beautiful beaches like Corbyn's Cove Beach, North Bay, Kalapathar Beach and more. A reliable source of the internet for a limited time as your handset will not get connectivity at most of the places.
    Know About Freedom struggle
    Visit Cellular Jail also known as Kaalapani and witness the light and sound show that will draw your memories back to the years of the freedom struggle
    Beautiful Havelock island
    Enjoy a Deluxe cruise ride to Havelock Island and explore the beauty of Elephant Beach and Radhanagar Beach which is recognised as the best beach in the Asia.
    Enjoy trip to Baratang Islands
    Visit Baratang Islands and Explore the Jarawa Boundaries through Thick Mangroves Here you will be visiting the Lime Stone Cave by 'Dongis' or small boats. The journey is sure to enthral you as it will take you underneath the dense forest of mangroves plantation.
    Ross and North Bay Islands | Land of the British Architecture
    North Bay also called “The Gateway to Port Blair” is famous for its coral reefs, exotic varieties of beautiful ornamental fishes and water sports activities. leaves you spellbound. North Bay is the perfect place to go snorkelling and also indulge in a glass-bottom boat ride, scuba diving and underwater sea walk.
    Deluxe Cruise transfer
    To visit Havelock Island Or Baratang Islands from Port Blair. you go via Govt. ferry / private cruise (Sea Link | Makruzz | ITT Majestic | Green Ocean
    Delicious Breakfast
    Enjoy Delicious breakfast at every stay with us.

    Andaman Tour package Itinerary

    Day 1 : AIRPORT PICK UP. PORT BLAIR | LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
  • Arrival at Port Blair in the morning/afternoon by flight and our representative will be waiting outside holding a placard with your name on it.
    • Port Blair
  • Next, go on a breathtaking trip to Cellular Jail which will draw your memories back to those years of the freedom struggle
    • Cellular Jail, Port Blair
  • After That you will enjoy the sound and light show Overnight stay at the hotel
    • Day 2 : TRIP FROM PORT BLAIR TO HAVELOCK IN CRUISE.
  • After breakfast, Depart from Port Blair to Havelock Island in a ferry. Upon reaching Havelock, our representative attends you and will drop you at your hotel.
    • Havelock Island trip
  • After reaching hotel have Lunch and enjoy sightseeing at Radhanagar beach and click memorable pictures of sunset.
    • Radhanagar Beach
  • Overnight stay at the Delux hotel at Havelock
    • Day 3 :Visit Elephant beach | Snorkeling & Water Activities at Beach
  • Enjoy Delicious breakfast at the hotel and set out for the trip
    • Elephant Beach
  • Embark on the most memorable trip to Elephant beach to witness the exotic elephant views.
  • You can also Explore the underwater life in an exciting adventure of Snorkeeling.
    • Snorkeling at Havelock
  • Later head back to the resort, Overnight stay at Havelock Island
    • Day 4 : Visit the Kalapathar Beach | Return to Port Blair by Ferry.
  • Enjoy Delicious breakfast & visit Kalapathar beach | Explore Kalapathar Beach and click beautiful pictures.
    • Kalapathar Beach
  • Return to Port blair via Inter island Cruise/ferry.
    • ferry to Port Blair
  • After reaching Port Blair, Our trip manager take you to your hotel. Overnight stay at port Blair.
    • Day 5 : Baratang Islands | Explore the Dense Green Tropical Rainforests.
  • After mouth-watering breakfast, you will depart for Baratang Islands. It is approximately 110 km away from Port Blair and is connected by ATR Road. Baratang Island has thick mangrove creeks on both sides followed by dense tropical forest.
    • Green Forest
  • You will then be visiting the Mud Volcanoes locally called ‘Jalki’. These volcanoes have erupted sporadically and squirt little fountains of mud.
    • Baratang
  • North Bay is the perfect place to go snorkelling and also indulge in a glass-bottom boat ride, scuba diving and underwater sea walk.
    • Day 6 : Visit the Ross and North Bay Islands
  • After Delicious breakfast, you will depart for Port Blair. The island is an ideal destination for nature walks amidst sylvan surroundings with deer’s, peacocks and exotic birds.
    • Ross island
  • North Bay is the perfect place to go snorkelling and also indulge in a glass-bottom boat ride, scuba diving and underwater sea walk.
    • Snorkeling at North Bay Island
  • Later in the evening return back to the hotel in Port Blair for an overnight stay.
    • Day 7 : Return with Loads of memories
  • After Delicious breakfast, you will depart for Veer Sawarkar Airport Via Private Cab.
