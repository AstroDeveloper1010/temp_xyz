    Scubadiving Highlights

    Discover Scubadiving in Andaman
    If you want to experience the magic of the underwater world, try one of our Intro To Discover Scuba Experience in Andaman. This programs is best for beginners and Non-swimmers. learn basics of scubadiving by PADI cerified Trainer and later enjoy dive with the trainer.
    Boat Dive in Andaman
    In this Package you will be taken to dive location via our Boat. you will learn basic scuba instructions and try scuba dive in sea with our Padi certified Trainer. You gear up on the boat and take a plunge into the waters. Boat diving takes the diving experience to an altogether different level.
    Know About PADI certified course
    In this package we provide full 4 day and 7 days Scubadiving Course in Andaman. Your Stay, Meals, Scuba Lessons & Boat Tours all included with this package. Trained professional divers assist you during the dive underwater and also while taking the plunge into the waters from the boat.
    Max Depth For Scubadiving & Timings
    The maximum depth is about 8-12 Mtr – how deep you want to go depends upon your comfort level and the instructor's discretion (we want you to have fun, but safety obviously comes first!). The duration of the dive depends on you, your air consumption and your comfort level. Our Beginners program lasts up to 45 Minutes excluding the training period and travel. The total duration may go up to 2 Hours.

    FAQ

    1. Do we require advanced bookings?
    Yes, pre-booking must be done in order to confirm your tour package Availability
    2. What if we arrive late at the dive Centre?
    By default, the trips leave at 8 am, but if you are coming on the ferry we'll try to schedule a different time slot. We recommend you to plan your inter-island Ferry trip carefully to hessle free experience in Andaman.
    3. Is it safe to Non-swimmers?
    We provide all our program in safe environment. Non-swimmers also can enjoy underwater experience, swimming is not required.
    4. How To Reach Andaman Islands?
    You can reach Andaman Islands by ship from The major ports which have ships that sail to Andamans are Chennai, Kolkata, and Vizag. Or by Flights. There is just one commercial airport in the islands, the Veer Savarkar Airport (IXZ) in Port Blair. We recommend travelers to reach by flight to Andaman as this is the most convenient way to reach the Andaman Islands. o
