    FAQ

    1. What are the best Places to visit in Goa?
    The best islands to visit are Arambol Beach, Mandrem Beach, Vegatore, Calangute-Baga in North Goa and Palolem, Colva, Benaulim, Biana, Vasco in South goa.
    2. What are the best Packages for Solo trip of Goa?
    if you are Coming on Weekends We Suggested you Book Weekend Trip Package. Although You can visit at any time but make sure to book before you leave and get Hassle-free Tour.
    3. What are the best Packages in Goa?
    If need to explore most Part of goa than you need at least 4-5 days. so you can travel with ease and freedom. You Can add & Remove on Scuba diving, snorkeling & Thrilling Watersports activities & available with discounted price in this tour package. We also provide Lowest Price option For Flights.
    4. What are the best Watersports activities to do in Goa?
    You can enjoy Snorkeling, Scuba Diving, Water Sports rides, Speed Boat, Parasailing, Jetski etc.
    5. Can Non-Swimmers do scuba diving?
    Frankly, there is no correlation between swimming and scuba diving. Swimming is the act of keeping ourselves above the water while scuba diving is done under the water than technically we don't need to know how to swim. Scuba diving does not require the use of the arms, just the fins (feet)
    6. What is the minimum age for scuba diving?
    The min. age is 14 years for scuba diving with us for complete safety purposes.
